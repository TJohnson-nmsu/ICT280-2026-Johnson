About Me & My Interests

Hi! I’m Torey Johnson. I’ve always been a die-hard Green Bay Packers fan — following their games, celebrating the wins, and analyzing their playoff runs has been a big part of my life. Football isn’t just a hobby for me; it’s a way to connect with friends and family while enjoying the thrill of the game.

Outside of sports, I’m really into computer science. I love learning how things work behind the scenes and creating small projects that challenge my problem-solving skills. Coding gives me a sense of accomplishment and keeps me thinking creatively.

I also enjoy staying active in other ways. Golf and rock climbing are two of my favorite activities because they push me to focus and improve steadily. On a completely different note, I’m passionate about culinary arts. Experimenting in the kitchen is one of my ways to unwind and express creativity.

Each of my interests, whether sports, tech, or hobbies, teaches me something new about myself. I try to combine fun with learning and personal growth in everything I do. Balancing all of this keeps my life interesting and rewarding.

If you’d like to know more about my projects, hobbies, or just connect, you can find links on my homepage. I’m always open to sharing experiences and learning from others.
