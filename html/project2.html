About Me and My Interests

Hi, I’m Torey. Football has been a huge part of my life for the past five years. After meeting my boyfriend, who is a die-hard Green Bay Packers fan, I quickly got hooked on the team, their history, and the excitement of every game. Before that, I mostly watched college football with my mom, who is a Tennessee Vols fan.

I am really into coding and cooking, and they actually feel pretty similar to me. Coding challenges me to think creatively and solve problems, while cooking lets me experiment and be patient. Both keep me focused and engaged, and they give me a real sense of accomplishment when something works out just right.

Golf and rock climbing are my favorite activities because they push me to focus and improve steadily. Climbing, in particular, challenges me mentally and physically, and I love the feeling of reaching the top. These activities balance my life and help me reset.

Now you know who I am. We can be friends!
